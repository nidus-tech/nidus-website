About Nidus

Today, we design industrial equipment for human operators. As fewer Americans choose to work in manufacturing, we need to automate factory operations.

Nidus builds adaptive, decision-making robotic systems that operate the high-precision machines that have powered factories for decades — starting with the defense industrial base, the largest buyer of US-made parts. Our goal is to reduce costs so the US can compete with China.

We’re based in New York City.

Why Join Now

  • Early. You’ll have enormous ownership and shape how we build.
  • Hard. Autonomous manipulation in real manufacturing environments is an unsolved problem.
  • Important. The defense industrial base needs modern manufacturing — and we’re building it. The next war will be won by logistics and this is the Knutson moment of the 21st century.
  • In-person. We work together in person. Great team, fast iteration, team lunch.

Who Thrives at Nidus

  • You’ve built systems that work outside the lab.
  • You care about physical-world performance, not just benchmarks.
  • You move quickly and prefer shipping over theorizing.
  • You want to work in-person with a small and senior technical staff. We think less is more these days.
